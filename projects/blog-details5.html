Kabul International Airport Waiting Hall & Parking Facility Project

  • Eng. Aziz
  • Jan 1, 2022
  • 12 Comments

The Kabul International Airport Waiting Hall and Parking Project is a major public–private partnership (DBOT) initiative aimed at modernizing and expanding passenger service infrastructure at Afghanistan’s busiest airport. The project is implemented under a 30-year concession agreement, including 2 years of construction, with a total investment of 400 million AFN, fully financed and executed by our company.

This project addresses a critical issue: passengers and visitors have long been forced to wait outdoors under harsh weather conditions, without access to a safe, comfortable, or dignified waiting facility.

Project Overview.

The project consists of a multi-level structure designed to provide comprehensive and modern services for airport users. The basement level is dedicated to organized vehicle parking, ensuring safe and efficient traffic management. The main level includes a spacious waiting hall along with commercial facilities such as shops, ticket offices, flower shops, and restaurants, creating a complete service environment for passengers and visitors. The upper level is designed to accommodate fully equipped VIP lounges for transit passengers, offering comfort, privacy, and facilities that meet international standards. The rooftop level features a landscaped green area and a café with a direct operational view of the airport runway and apron, providing a unique and modern experience.

Project Objective.

The primary objective of this project is to establish a modern, weather-protected waiting facility that provides a dignified and comfortable environment for passengers and greeters. In addition, the project aims to develop a high-capacity and well-organized parking system to improve traffic flow and safety around the airport. It also seeks to create a comprehensive commercial service zone that enhances passenger convenience through the availability of essential services such as shops, ticketing, and dining. Furthermore, the development includes VIP and transit passenger facilities designed to meet international standards of comfort and privacy. The project also focuses on enhancing the overall passenger experience by incorporating a rooftop green area and café with views of airport operations. Another key objective is to reduce congestion at airport entrances through structured infrastructure, while also generating sustainable revenue through commercial spaces and improving the overall image and functionality of Kabul International Airport.

Project Significance.

This project represents a major step toward improving airport infrastructure and passenger services in Afghanistan. By combining functionality, modern design, and commercial integration, it contributes to a more organized, efficient, and user-friendly airport environment. The facility is designed to meet both current demands and future growth, positioning it as a long-term asset that supports the development and modernization of Kabul International Airport.
