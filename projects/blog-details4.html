Daman Passenger Terminal Project – Kandahar Province

  • Eng. Aziz
  • Feb 7, 2023

The Daman Passenger Terminal Project, located in the Nesaji Bridge area of Daman District, Kandahar Province, is one of the region’s most important transportation infrastructure developments. Designed as a centralized and fully equipped passenger terminal, the project aims to organize and improve the movement of travelers and vehicles across Kandahar and its surrounding provinces. Strategically positioned near the historic Nesaji Bridge, the terminal provides convenient access for all categories of transport, including private vehicles, mid-size transport units, and large passenger buses. The project reflects a strong commitment to enhancing public transportation systems, improving safety, and supporting economic mobility in southern Afghanistan.

The Daman Passenger Terminal Project represents a transformative step toward creating a modern, organized, and efficient transportation hub in Kandahar Province.

Project Overview.

The development consists of a modern terminal facility designed to handle high passenger volumes while ensuring smooth traffic flow and operational efficiency. The project includes organized waiting areas for passengers, administrative and operational buildings, and clearly defined parking zones for different types of vehicles. Internal road networks and controlled entry and exit points have been carefully planned to reduce congestion and improve accessibility. In addition, the facility incorporates essential public services, safety infrastructure, drainage systems, and environmental improvements, creating a complete and functional transportation hub that meets modern infrastructure standards.

Project Objective.

The primary objective of this project is to establish a well-organized and standardized passenger terminal that improves the overall travel experience for the public. It aims to provide a central and efficient hub for passenger vehicles, reducing congestion and enhancing traffic management within Kandahar Province. The project also focuses on improving safety, accessibility, and comfort for travelers while supporting regional connectivity and facilitating smoother inter-provincial transportation. Furthermore, it contributes to the development of a sustainable infrastructure asset under the Ministry of Transport and Civil Aviation, strengthening the long-term capacity of Afghanistan’s transportation network.

Project Significance.

The Daman Passenger Terminal represents a major advancement in the modernization of transportation infrastructure in Kandahar Province. By introducing an organized and efficient system for passenger movement, the project helps reduce urban congestion and provides a safer and more structured environment for daily travelers. It plays a key role in supporting economic activity by improving mobility and accessibility, while also strengthening regional connectivity across southern Afghanistan. Designed to meet both current demands and future growth, the terminal stands as a long-term infrastructure asset that enhances the overall quality and functionality of public transportation services.
